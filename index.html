Channel Name: Sixfold Legion

Who Are We:

We are a group of student e-games players.

What We Do:

Our focus is all about game-exploring, playing different games such as FPS, sci-fi, adventure, and more. We intend to create a montage video featuring highlights and bloopers from our gaming experiences.

What We Plan to Build:

We aspire to create a game-explorer community that brings joy and excitement to gaming enthusiasts. Our platform will serve as a virtual world where we explore different games, engage in conversations, and connect with others who are interested in the things we do.

How We Promote Our Channel:

We plan to promote our streaming through visually appealing posters that convey our streaming schedule and chosen games that we will be playing. For example, an arcade-themed poster announcing our mobile game streams every Saturday and Sunday from Philippine Server time(PHT) 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM and the games that would be involved in the gameplay such Roblox, Mobile legends, Call of duty. The poster shall encourage viewers to join our stream.
